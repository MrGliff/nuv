School of Science

School of Liberal Studies and Education  

The School of Liberal Studies & Education at Navrachana University takes an interdisciplinary approach to learning and through its varied course offerings prepares students to succeed in media organisations, academic institutions, or work in the development sector with rural non-profits or international agencies.

Working in close association with the industry, and led by industry-experienced faculty members, it is one of the premium institutes in Gujarat. The School of Liberal Studies & Education offers programs in Journalism and Mass Communication (BAJMC), Social Work (MSW) and Education (BEd, MEd, PhD, PGD ECCE).

School of Environmental Design and Architecture  

SEDA's dynamic and challenging curriculum is led by the school's highly accomplished faculty and guest lecturers, who are also working professionals and experts in their field of specialization. Student's creativity and critical thinking capabilities are enriched through extensive design investigations and explorations, to not just expand their knowledge, but also to confront the complexities of the modern built environment.

The Bachelor's in Architecture and Design (Interior/ Landscape) are broad-based degree programs combining technology, theory and design. Students work on a range of different design projects, with studio sessions and study tours facilitating design dialogue and cross pollination of ideas amongst peers and faculty.
