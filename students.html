Academic Calendar

The academic calendar is a systematic schedule of all the major events that occur in an academic year. These events majorly include the dates of semester commencements, important examinations, distributions of hall tickets, the announcement of results, registration of re-examination, Diwali break, etc. The academic details of students serves as a useful tool to help students get a clear understanding of events that would happen during the year and makes sure that they occur as per the plan, without the need for repeated communication.

Holiday List

The Holiday List provides a comprehensive overview of all scheduled national and public holidays. It serves as a valuable resource for students, faculty, and staff, outlining important dates and enabling efficient planning. By offering a chronological schedule, it ensures everyone is well-prepared for upcoming breaks and festivities.

Examination

An evaluation of the knowledge acquired during the preceding semester serves as a vital measure to monitor individual progress, primarily conducted through end-of-semester examinations. Although mid-semester timetables may differ across educational institutions, the schedule for end-of-semester exams remains consistent. This schedule encompasses essential events such as hall ticket distribution and the dissemination of crucial announcements pertaining to any examination-related matters. These comprehensive updates empower students to proactively ready themselves for their final assessments, ensuring they are well-prepared and informed.
